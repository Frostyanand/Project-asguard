# 📖 Overview

ASGUARD (**AI Smart Guardian**) is an AI-powered Smart Home Energy Management Platform designed to help homeowners monitor, analyze, predict, and optimize electricity consumption.

The platform combines Artificial Intelligence, Digital Twin Technology, historical energy simulation, cloud computing, and interactive analytics to provide appliance-level insights and intelligent recommendations for improving energy efficiency.

Unlike traditional monitoring systems that only display electricity usage, ASGUARD goes beyond monitoring by helping users understand energy consumption patterns, predict future usage, identify energy wastage, and automate energy-saving actions.

The platform enables users to visualize their home through an interactive Digital Twin, analyze historical energy records, simulate real-time energy consumption, and receive AI-powered recommendations for reducing electricity costs while promoting sustainable living.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Modern households often face several challenges in managing electricity consumption:

- Monthly electricity bills provide no appliance-level insights.
- Difficult to identify which devices consume the most power.
- Lack of predictive analysis for future electricity consumption.
- No intelligent recommendations for reducing energy usage.
- Limited visualization of household energy consumption.
- Existing systems focus on monitoring rather than decision support.
- Users remain unaware of inefficient energy consumption patterns.

These limitations often result in unnecessary electricity costs, energy wastage, and reduced efficiency.

---

# 💡 Our Solution

ASGUARD addresses these challenges through an intelligent cloud-based platform that combines Artificial Intelligence, Digital Twin technology, historical simulation, and advanced analytics.

The platform enables users to:

- Monitor appliance-level electricity usage.
- Visualize their smart home using a Digital Twin.
- Analyze historical energy consumption.
- Predict future electricity demand.
- Receive AI-powered recommendations.
- Detect abnormal energy usage.
- Reduce electricity bills.
- Improve overall household energy efficiency.
